How to convert image to text?

  1. Upload an image, e.g a PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, TIFF, BMP, PNM or JFIF.
  2. Select the language of the text in your image.
  3. After clicking on "Upload" you can check your extracted text

Free Service

This OCR service is free for "Guest" users (without registration).

Use OCR online

To extract text and characters from scanned PDF documents (including multipage files), photos and digital camera captured images.

Multiple language support

The image-to-text ocr provides multilingual support. It can translate images into text in more than 5 languages.

Copy to clipboard

You can copy the converted text to the clipboard and paste it into the desired file or directory.

Download text file

Data can be lost or misplaced if not saved appropriately. You can directly download converted text as a file instead of copying the text.
